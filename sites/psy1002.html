Cognitive Psychology 1

This module is required for Artificial Intelligence students. It is fully theoretically and focuses on different topics such as perception, attention, perfomance, psychophysics, decision making and cognitive neuroscience. This module can seem very boring and just memorising stuff, but if you really focus on the videos then it gets interesting. You learn about how people function and understand why so. This knowledge comes in very handy if you wanted to develope your own AI.
My personal favorite part was the cognitive neuroscience part. We learned about human brain, how we use different machines to have a look into our brains and so on. I was very interested in the EEG, which I have also pictured, because it can be used to now reconstruct images on what you see. This can be implemented with people who are verbally unable to communicate.
