Why I chose Sheffield

I chose Sheffield because of a variety of reasons.

  1. I like the modules they were offering
  2. They have a good league table ranking domestically and internationally
  3. Their student union is the best in the country
  4. They have a lot of opportunities for students to work on their own projects
  5. I really liked the city
  6. Their graduate prospect was really good
  7. A lot of teamwork, which is good for CV

How is my course so far?

It is really great honestly, I am really happy I chose Sheffield, because even if there is a lot of work,
the modules make it enjoyable. I really like what we are doing in each module and that makes the course for me very enjoyable.
